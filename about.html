Our Story

How Dosa Craft started, and why every dosa is made with care.

How It Started

Dosa Craft began with a simple idea: dosa should still feel comforting, but it should never feel predictable. We keep the heart of South Indian cooking intact and build on it with fresh ingredients, thoughtful combinations, and a menu that gives every kind of diner something to look forward to.

This is a place for the masala dosa loyalist, the cheese dosa explorer, the family ordering for everyone, and the friend who always wants to try the signature dish first.

The result is a South Indian fusion restaurant in Indore that respects the original dish while giving people more ways to enjoy it, whether they come in for a classic favorite or a bold fusion signature.

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Dosa Craft different from a regular South Indian restaurant?

We use a traditional, authentic South Indian batter as our canvas, but we fill it with creative, modern, and globally-inspired ingredients ranging from rich cheeses to Schezwan spices. It's the perfect mix of classic crunch and new flavors.

Where can I find the best dosa in Indore?

You can find Dosa Craft at 50 Bada Sarafa Chaupati in Indore. We are famous locally for our unique menu and premium ingredients, especially our viral Cheese Volcano Dosa.

Do you serve Jain food at Dosa Craft?

Yes! We have a dedicated section of Jain specials, including the Jain Paneer Jinny Dosa and Jain Corn Cheese Dosa, prepared strictly according to dietary guidelines.

Is Dosa Craft good for breakfast or late-night dining?

Absolutely. A light, crispy dosa is the perfect start to your day, and our indulgent cheese and butter-loaded fusion dosas make for an incredible late-night street food experience in Indore.
