Franchise Opportunity

Dosa Craft is expanding nationally. We are looking for franchise partners to carry the brand forward in high-footfall locations across India.

Partner with Dosa Craft.

Dosa Craft is a South Indian fusion food brand based in Indore, expanding its network nationally through the franchise route. The program is designed to help franchise partners operate outlets efficiently while maintaining industry benchmark standards for food quality and service.

Begin Your Journey

Why Join Dosa Craft?

The franchise program focuses on Mall Format Express outlets and kiosk business formats, designed for high-footfall locations. Franchise partners benefit from an established brand, a standardized supply and quality system, and dedicated operational support.

Standardized Taste & Quality

Food quality and taste are standardized through the Master Kitchen system, ensuring consistency across all locations without complex on-site preparation.

Broad Customer Appeal

The concept targets multiple demographic groups — students, young families, and customers across medium and lower-middle income segments.

Delivery Platform Tie-Ups

Dosa Craft has tie-ups with leading food delivery platforms including Zomato, Swiggy, and Uber Eats to expand customer reach beyond the outlet.

Faster Establishment

Operating under an established brand with a specially curated menu helps franchise partners grow faster compared to starting an independent restaurant.

Full Franchise Support

From setup and staff training to marketing campaigns and ongoing operational guidance, Dosa Craft provides end-to-end support for every franchise partner.

Specially Curated Menu

The menu is designed by the company to balance familiar South Indian taste with modern fusion ideas — and is continuously updated through ongoing R&D.

How We Support You

Dosa Craft provides strong operational and strategic support to help every franchise location run smoothly and maintain the brand's quality standards.

Setup Support

Layout planning, equipment selection, initial staff training, and launch activities are all covered as part of the franchise setup.

Staffing Assistance

Support with identifying and recruiting staff, along with brand-standard training. If staff are unavailable due to emergencies, alternate staff can be arranged within 48 hours.

Operational Guidance

Continuous transfer of management expertise, troubleshooting support, and operational guidance to help you maintain consistent standards.

Marketing & Branding

National advertising campaigns across radio, newspaper, and digital channels, along with PR activities and promotions starting 15–20 days before opening.

Menu Innovation

Continuous research and development to introduce new menu items that keep the offering fresh and relevant to customer preferences.

Technology & Billing

Franchise outlets use computerized billing systems recommended by Dosa Craft, along with software to manage sales, revenue, and business projections.

Your Responsibilities

As a franchise partner, you represent Dosa Craft in your location. Here is what that involves.

01

Location & Setup

Identify and secure suitable premises, set up the outlet according to brand specifications, and install the required security and billing systems.

02

Daily Operations

Manage daily outlet operations, handle upfront investments, and procure raw materials from local markets using local logistics systems.

03

Quality Standards

Ensure high food and service quality standards are maintained at all times, in line with Dosa Craft's brand guidelines.

04

Local Marketing

Manage local sales and promotional activities — including stalls near colleges, print advertising, and happy hour offers — to build a customer base in your area.

📋

Mandatory Customer Notice

All franchise outlets must prominently display: "Do not pay if you don't get a computerized bill."
