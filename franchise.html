Why Join Dosa Craft?

Dosa Craft is rapidly expanding its network nationally through the franchise route. Our program focuses on Mall Format Express outlets and kiosk business formats, purposefully designed for high-footfall locations to ensure maximum reach and profitability.

Standardized Taste & Quality

Our Master Kitchen system guarantees that the taste and quality of our food remain consistent across all locations without complex on-site preparation.

Broad Customer Appeal

Targeting multiple demographic groups—from young students to families. We offer food and pricing perfectly suited to medium and lower-middle-income segments.

Delivery Partnerships

Direct strategic alliances and national tie-ups with leading food delivery platforms like Zomato, Swiggy, and Uber Eats.

Faster Establishment

Operating under an established brand name with specially curated menus helps you grow much faster compared to starting an independent restaurant.

High ROI Potential

Benefit from our highly profitable business model built for fast breakeven and sustained long-term revenue growth in high-footfall areas.

Specially Curated Menu

Deliver a dining experience tailored to the local palate while maintaining our core brand signature, balancing classic tastes and modern fusion.

Setup Support

Staffing Assistance

Operational Guidance

Continuous management expertise transfer, systemic troubleshooting, and sustained operational support to maximize your ROI.

Marketing & Branding
